Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

mail-hook is a personal email automation tool that reads Gmail messages authorized by the user, generates summaries and classifications, and sends Discord notifications.

Information processed

  • Email subject lines, sender information, snippets, body text, thread information, and attachment metadata.
  • Operational metadata such as message IDs, processing status, runtime history IDs, and error records.

How information is used

  • To detect new Gmail messages.
  • To summarize and classify messages for notification delivery.
  • To send Discord notifications requested by the user.
  • To maintain deduplication, retry, and operational monitoring records.

Third-party services

  • Google Gmail API is used to access authorized Gmail data.
  • OpenAI API may process sanitized message content to generate summaries and classifications.
  • Discord Webhook receives formatted notification payloads.
  • Google Cloud Firestore stores runtime state, sender history, processed message records, and error events.

Data retention

Operational records may be retained in Firestore for reliability, deduplication, history, and troubleshooting. Data may be updated or removed by the operator as needed.

User choices

Users may revoke Gmail access for this application at any time through their Google Account permissions settings. Revoking access prevents the app from reading Gmail data going forward.
